VERBAL ABILITY (Advanced)

Reading Passage

Databases are at the heart of modern applications. Efficient storage,
retrieval, and management of data are essential for building scalable
software products. Companies rely on well-designed databases to ensure
data consistency and performance.

Questions

1. Why are databases important in modern applications?
Ans:Databases are important because they enable efficient storage, retrieval, and management of data, which is essential for building scalable and high-performance software systems.

2. What does data consistency mean?
Ans:Data consistency means that the data remains accurate, reliable, and uniform across the database, even after multiple operations like updates or transactions.

3. Why do companies need well-designed databases?
Ans:Companies need well-designed databases to ensure data consistency, improve performance, and support scalability in their applications.
