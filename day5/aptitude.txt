APTITUDE (Speed, Time & Distance)

Question 1
A train travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is its speed in m/s?
Soln:

d=60 km, t=1 hr 
Speed in km/h=60
60×5/18=16.67 m/s

Ans: 16.67 m/s

Question 2
A car travels at 40 km/h for 2 hours and 60 km/h for next 2 hours.
Find average speed.
Ans:
   40 km/h for 2 hr 
   d=80 km
   60 km/h for 2 hr
   d=120 km
Total distance=200 km
Total time=4 hr

Speed=200/4=50 km/h
     =50 km/h

Ans: 50 km/h

Question 3
Two trains moving in opposite directions at 50 km/h and 70 km/h.
Find relative speed.
Ans:
  Opposite Direction → Add speeds

     50+70=120 km/h

Ans: 120 km/h

Question 4
A person walks at 5 km/h and runs at 10 km/h.
Find time taken to cover 15 km if half distance is walked and half is run.

Soln:
     Total d=15 km
     Half=7.5 km

Walk time:7.5/5=1.5 hr
Run time:7.5/10=0.75 hr

Time:1.5+0.75=2.25 hr

Ans: 2.25 hours(2 hr 15 min)

Question 5
If a train crosses a pole in 10 seconds and its length is 100 meters,
find its speed.
Soln:
   speed= d/t
100/10=10 m/s
Convert to km/h:
10×18/5=36 km/h

Ans: 36 km/h
