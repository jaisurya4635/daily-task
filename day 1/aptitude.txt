1.Question 1
A number increased by 20% becomes 120.
Find the original number.

120%100=1.20
1.20*x=120
x=120/1.20
x=100
Answer :100

Question 2
If the price of a laptop increases from ₹50,000 to ₹60,000,
what is the percentage increase ?

increase = 60000-50000 = 10000
percentage = (increase/original price) *100
=(10000/50000)*100
=20%
Answer :20%
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Question 3
A student scored 72 out of 90 in an exam.
Find the percentage score.

Percentage = (Mark obtained/total Marks)*100
=(72/90)*100
=0.8 *100
=80%
Answer :80%
